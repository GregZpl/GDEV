Welcome to Redshift Interactive

Redshift Interactive features stunning original 19th-century wooden-floored Main Room and slate-floored Stone Room with original paint peeling atmospherically from the walls. Colorful cast-iron columns throughout both spaces provide organic industrial backdrops at every turn.

Our striking pallet wall was constructed using over forty pallets to create a highly regarded backdrop—widely used in film and photography—which offers incredible texture, bronzed wood, and abundant natural light from the original windows beside it.
